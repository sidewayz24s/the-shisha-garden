Our Story

An Exotic Escape in the Heart of Edgware

Welcome to The Shisha Garden™, an exclusive destination unlike any other in London. Nestled within The Madonna Halley Hotel, we offer our members an exotic setting and a unique ambiance designed for relaxation and indulgence. Our vibrant, colourful interiors and lush decor create a truly unforgettable experience, far removed from typical shisha lounges.

We pride ourselves on a fusion of authentic Mediterranean dishes, crafted from locally sourced ingredients, alongside a wide selection of premium shisha flavours. Complement your experience with our homemade desserts, refreshing milkshakes, and expertly mixed cocktails, all bursting with authentic tastes.
